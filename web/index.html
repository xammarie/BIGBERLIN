actions

pick what NoWork should do.

one tap, one input. no chat boxes, no prompts. the action is the prompt.

complete
finishes the questions you didn’t get to. leaves your right answers untouched.
correct
fixes wrong answers, leaves the right ones alone.
fill out
every blank, every box, in one pass.
annotate
margin notes, underlines, hints in your hand.
restyle
rewrites existing handwriting in any style from your library.
how it works

three steps. no chat, no prompts.

we hate writing prompts as much as you hate writing homework.

01
snap the page

one or many pages of the worksheet. the camera is the input.

02
add your hand once

scribble a sample of your handwriting. we keep a small private library you can switch between.

03
pick an action

complete, correct, fill out, annotate, or restyle. get the pages back ready to print or hand in.

built

made in 24 hours.

at bigberlinhack 2026 — donaustraße 44, berlin.

a swift app on top of supabase edge functions. gemini 2.5 reads the worksheet and reasons. gpt-image-2 renders the answers in your hand. hera generates short video walkthroughs when you want to learn the answer, not just see it.

submission requires three partner technologies. we use four — gemini, hera, tavily, and supabase. plus aikido for security.
